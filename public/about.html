About Me

My name is Samrawit Hailu Tadesse, and I reside in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Currently, I work as a lecturer in the Department of Architecture at the Federal TVET Institute (FTVTI). I hold both a BSc degree in Civil Engineering and Architectural Design Technology from a recognized university, which has equipped me with a solid foundation in technical knowledge and problem-solving skills. Additionally, I recently completed my Master's degree in Environmental Architecture from Adama Science and Technology University (ASTU).

Over the years, my passion for web development has grown steadily, and I am determined to pursue this field as I believe it offers endless possibilities for creativity, problem-solving, and innovation. I am fascinated by the way web development combines technology, design, and user experience to create interactive and visually appealing websites.

In the next ten years, I envision myself as a highly skilled fullstack webpage developer contributing to innovative projects that push the boundaries of technology in architectural design and sustainable development. With this scholarship, I will have the means to achieve my goals and make a meaningful impact on society by creating user-friendly and environmentally conscious web applications.
